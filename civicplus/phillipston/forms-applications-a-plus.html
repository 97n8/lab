Town of Phillipston, Massachusetts

Forms & Applications

Use this page to find common Town forms by department. Download the form, review the instructions, and submit it with any required signatures, fees, plans, or supporting materials.

How to Submit a Form

  • Follow the instructions printed on the form.
  • Some applications require original signatures, fees, plans, inspections, or board review.
  • Contact the department before submitting if you are unsure which form applies.

Before You Start

Forms may be updated from time to time. These links open the Town Document Center so residents can access the current posted version.
